# Research Agenda

1. What did the two earlier City DPR studies conclude, and which assumptions are now obsolete?
2. How much reclaimed water is reliably available by month after contractual and environmental obligations?
3. What contaminants and concentrations are present in Flagstaff influent, reclaimed water, receiving environments, and biosolids?
4. What AWT train would ADEQ accept for Flagstaff, with and without RO?
5. What is the firm yield and delivered cost of the first 1, 2, and 4 MGD of AWT?
6. What is the current RGR cost, schedule, treatment requirement, and funding plan?
7. What are the energy and brine-disposal requirements for each alternative?
8. Which grants or federal programs are realistically available, on what schedule, and with what match?
9. How should reclaimed-water uses be prioritized during summer?
10. What pilot project would reduce the most uncertainty at the lowest cost?
