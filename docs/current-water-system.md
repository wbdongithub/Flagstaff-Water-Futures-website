# Current Water System

## Potable sources

Flagstaff draws from local wells, the Lake Mary wellfield, Woody Mountain wells, Upper Lake Mary, and Inner Basin wells and springs. Source use varies with hydrology, availability, repair needs, and cost.

## Wastewater and reclaimed water

Wildcat Hill and Rio de Flag treated about 1.97 billion gallons of wastewater influent in 2024. The plants produced about 1.89 billion gallons of treated effluent. Approximately 622 million gallons were delivered through reclaimed systems and 1.265 billion gallons were discharged.

## Why these numbers matter

The discharged volume is not a guaranteed AWT supply: seasonal demand, treatment capacity, environmental flows, contracts, and operational constraints must be evaluated. But it is a large local water stream and deserves a formal availability analysis.
