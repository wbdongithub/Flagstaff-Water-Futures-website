# Different Risks Require Different Responses

- **Supply risk:** drought, wildfire, source decline, mechanical outage.
- **Public-health risk:** pathogens and chronic chemical exposure.
- **Environmental risk:** discharge effects, groundwater drawdown, brine, energy, and habitat.
- **Financial risk:** cost escalation, low initial utilization, grant failure, and rate impacts.
- **Schedule risk:** permitting, partnerships, right-of-way, design, and construction.
- **Technology risk:** underperformance, operator burden, and replacement needs.

The alternatives should be compared as portfolios of risk, not on a single favored metric.
