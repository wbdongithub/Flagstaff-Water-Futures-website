# A Fair Alternatives Analysis

A formal comparison should use the same planning horizon, financing assumptions, energy prices, water yield, contingency, inflation basis, and reliability standard for both alternatives.

## Minimum criteria

- Firm annual and peak-day water yield
- Capital cost and timing of expenditures
- Annual operating and replacement cost
- Energy per acre-foot delivered
- Treatment reliability and source-water risk
- Salt and brine disposal
- Environmental and cultural impacts
- Grant, federal, regional, and local funding probability
- Ratepayer burden
- Time until useful water is delivered
- Expandability and stranded-asset risk
