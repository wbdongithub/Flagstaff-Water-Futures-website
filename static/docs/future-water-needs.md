# Why Plan for More Water?

Water infrastructure must be planned long before a shortage. The need for a next increment may arise from several overlapping concerns: population growth, source decline, drought, wildfire vulnerability, aging facilities, emergency redundancy, and changing water-quality expectations.

FWG's emphasis is on **the next increment**, not necessarily a single forever project. AWT could provide an earlier, phased increment while RGR remains a potential later or regional supply. That proposition needs engineering analysis rather than assumption.
