# How Projects Actually Get Built

Engineering merit is necessary but not sufficient. Projects require political support, regulatory approval, public confidence, staff capacity, and a financeable structure.

## RGR funding ecosystem

RGR's scale likely requires federal appropriations or Bureau of Reclamation participation, tribal and regional partnerships, state support, and local debt or rate revenue. These partnerships can make a major project possible, but also add time, conditions, and uncertainty.

## AWT funding ecosystem

AWT may fit water reuse, drought resilience, pilot, demonstration, research, WIFA/SRF, WIFIA, and local bond pathways. A first module may be small enough to finance differently from an ultimate buildout.

The probability and timing of outside funding should be treated as part of project risk, not as free money.
